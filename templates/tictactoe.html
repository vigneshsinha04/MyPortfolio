Tic-Tac-Toe Game

Tic-tac-toe, or noughts and crosses, or Xs and Os is a paper-and-pencil game for two players, X and O, who take turns marking the spaces in a 3Ã—3 grid. The player who succeeds in placing three of their marks in a horizontal, vertical, or diagonal row is the winner.

Here I have developed a simple game of classic Tic-Tac-Toe using Python. This game is played by two players X and O. By default, X will start the game.

Some interesting facts of game combinations when X starts the game.

  • 91 distinct positions are won by (X)
  • 44 distinct positions are won by (O)
  • 3 distinct positions are drawn (often called a "cat's game")
